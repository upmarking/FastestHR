Build a complete SaaS HRMS platform called **FastestHR**.

The product should help companies manage the entire employee lifecycle in one platform.

There should be **three main user roles**:

1. **Company Admin (Founder / HR Head / Leadership)**
2. **HR Manager**
3. **Employee**

The platform must include **three separate dashboards** for each role.

The UI should be modern, fast, minimal and mobile responsive.

---

### CORE PRODUCT MODULES

Build the platform with the following modules.

### 1. Recruitment Management

• Job posting creation
• Career page for companies
• Applicant tracking system (ATS)
• Resume parsing
• Candidate pipeline stages
• Interview scheduling
• Interview feedback forms
• Offer letter generation

---

### 2. Employee Onboarding

• Digital onboarding workflow
• Document upload & verification
• Digital signing of offer letters
• Automated onboarding checklist
• Welcome dashboard for new hires

---

### 3. Employee Database

• Complete employee profiles
• Department & role management
• Organization chart
• Employee document vault

---

### 4. Attendance & Time Tracking

• Daily attendance tracking
• Clock in / clock out
• Remote work tagging
• Shift management
• Late / early leave tracking

---

### 5. Leave Management

• Leave request system
• Leave approval workflow
• Leave balance tracking
• Holiday calendar
• Leave analytics

---

### 6. Payroll Management

• Salary structure configuration
• Automated monthly payroll generation
• Payslip generation
• Tax deduction management
• Bonus / incentives

---

### 7. Performance Management

• Goal setting (OKRs)
• Quarterly reviews
• Performance scoring
• Manager feedback
• 360 degree reviews

---

### 8. Employee Self Service Portal

Employees should be able to:

• Apply for leave
• View payslips
• Update profile information
• Download HR documents
• Submit requests

---

### 9. HR Helpdesk

• Employees can raise HR tickets
• HR team can respond
• Ticket categories (payroll / leave / benefits / IT)

---

### 10. Announcement & Internal Communication

• Company announcements
• Policy updates
• Internal posts

---

### 11. Document Management

• HR policy documents
• NDA / contracts
• Employee agreements
• Document expiry reminders

---

### 12. Employee Exit Management

• Resignation submission
• Exit interview forms
• Asset return tracking
• Final settlement

---

### 13. AI HR Assistant

Include an AI assistant that can:

• Answer HR questions
• Explain policies
• Help employees navigate the system

---

### 14. Analytics Dashboard

Admin dashboard should show:

• Total employees
• Hiring statistics
• Attrition rate
• Leave statistics
• Payroll costs

Include charts and insights.

---

### 15. Role Based Access Control

Ensure different access levels:

Admin
HR
Manager
Employee
